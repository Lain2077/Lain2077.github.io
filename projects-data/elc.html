Earth's Last Computer

[SPRING 2022]
LINK TO ELC (Computer only 0_0)

Art Contexte is an annual event that takes place at Cégep de Saint-Laurent to promote various art forms and kinds of performances. Earth`s Last Computer was presented during the 2022 edition.

As an interactive fiction with no instructions, you're invited to consult what's known as the latest computer on earth, with remains of past historical artifacts that survived a cataclysmic event leading to a mass extinction.
