DÉLUGE

[AUTUMN 2023]

For each of Le Culte's editions, the audio conception team produces a small EP for which our team needs to produce an accompanied video-clip for each chapters. Being assigned two chapters, with first versions received about a month and a half prior to the event, Marianne and I planned that we were going for an hyper-realistic style production. It is after listening to the first versions and considering the wished bell curved climax that we chose to do the third and last songs of the ep.

DÉLUGE was of course all about chaos and turbulence, but we couldn't remove the flashy and explicit images from our heads. We therefore went all in on a straight DÉLUGE by all means.

This dual-episode project was produced in a short time frame as a first real full-dive into Unreal Engine. Prior to this project, I had little to no knowledge on UE's inner workings.

Features used:

  • Chaos Cache
  • Fluid Sim
  • Trigger events
  • Camera Keyframing
  • General assets management
