Entre deux stations

[AUTUMN 2024]

Entre deux stations was showcased during Minute Mapp, a special event within MAPP MTL, an international projection mapping festival based in Montreal.

The theme of this year was perspective. Prior to developing the concept, we expressed an interest in using mixed media. From this initial idea, we arrived at a metro-related concept, which required modeling the train in Blender to match the scale of the wall's doors.

In the end, Entre deux stations reflects those small, mundane moments in life that, depending on your perspective, can be experienced in a panoply of different ways.

Features used:

  • Modeling and UV Mapping (Blender)
  • PostProcess Materials (Shaders)
  • Chaos
  • Niagara Particles
