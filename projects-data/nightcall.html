Night Call

[FALL 2024]

Night Call is a short film produced with the goal in mind of familiarizing myself with Unreal's available features to achieve an hyperrealistic look as well as skeletal meshes rigging. The project was completed in a span of about a month, with a workflow including a paper storyboard and animatics.

The car has been rigged from zero, integrating all basics functions you would expect from a car rig as well as suspension and wheel and pedals controls. Night Call also permitted me to go deeper into metahumans manual animation.

In order to overcome a major issue with Unreal's stock camera regarding depth of field, I had to dig into ways of giving it an anamorphic distortion to obtain a more cinematic and appealing look. This project involved a whole load of Lumen debugging around emissive and transluscent materials in order to get rid of visual artifacts throwing us out from immersion.

Features used:

  • Unreal's Rigging system
  • Camera lens profiles
  • Metahumans
  • Niagara systems & caching
