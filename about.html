About Me

Hi! I'm Edo, currently studying Interactive Media at UQAM. I arrived in Montreal at a very young age with my parents. Montreal is the city I consider my roots.

Like many others, I don’t exactly know what I want to do for the rest of my life, but I’m open to wherever I land, as long as the people around me are kind and considerate. Coming from a cinema background, I wasn’t sure if Interactive Media would align with my interests upon entering the program, but I quickly realized that what matters to me is learning new things. Interactive Media combines many of my passions, making it a good fit for who I am.

I've always been curious about everything, constantly seeking answers to things I don’t understand. I’ve also traveled quite a lot, which helps me grow by exposing me to different cultures, ways of living, and opinions.

Speaking of opinions, I’m very open to understanding anyone’s point of view, whether we agree or not. I believe everyone has a reason for their beliefs and that we all want to live a happy and peaceful life.
